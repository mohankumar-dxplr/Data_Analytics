## Segment Descriptions

| Segment | Description | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Champions** | Bought recently, often, and spend most | Reward & nurture |
| **Loyal** | Regular customers, good spend | Upsell & cross-sell |
| **Potential** | Moderate recency/frequency/spend | Grow relationship |
| **At Risk** | Used to buy but haven't recently | Re-engage campaigns |
| **Lost** | Long time since last purchase, low spend | Win-back or ignore |
